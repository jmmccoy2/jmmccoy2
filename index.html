About

Jonathan McCoy is a Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. He enjoys long walks at the lake, fishing with his friend, playing hockey at the Shell, and taking care of his plants. He hopes to make a ton of cash when he’s an adult and has huge dreams of owning at least two phones and two homes. If he doesn’t succeed at his primary goal, he has an alternative plan of working as a professional fisherman in a foreign country and living off the food he catches. Jon is a simple man, and he lives by the motto of go big or go home.
